BIOGRAPHY

With a passion for photography since young, Ming is a photographer at heart, aimed at capturing moments, telling stories and deepening connections through his visuals.


Based in Singapore, he started photography in 2017 and was a student photographer for over 5 years, gaining tremendous experience in various mediums, such as sports, events, concerts, street and landscape, A testament to his work quality would be his consistent features in school social media posts, banners and yearbooks, photo exhibitions like the Ngee Ann Photographic Exhibition, and achieving 'Honorable Mention' for his submission in the 2022 Our Schools Our Stories Photo Competition organised by the Ministry of Education. Having led as the Club Chairman, he not only has the technical abilities, but also the skills to coordinate and lead a group of photographers to cover events.

Throughout the years, he has consistently honed his craft and developed his own visual style. Being well versed in covering events, he now works as a freelance photographer/videographer, offering services such as sport, concert, personal and commercial event coverage, photography and videography workshops and more.
